Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Grease a pan with butter and line it with parchment paper
  3. Combine rolled oats, puffed brown rice cereal, almonds, coconut, cocoa powder, salt, and cayenne in a bowl
  4. Combine brown sugar, maple syrup, vegetable oil, and water in a small saucepan and bring to a simmer over medium-high heat
  5. Stir, and let the mixture boil for 1 minute
  6. Pour sugar mixture over the dry ingredients in the bowl and mix thoroughly
  7. Transfer to the prepared pan, and smooth out the top, pressing lightly with a spatula
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes
  9. While still hot, use a spatula to score the pan into 8 portions. Let cool to room temperature before breaking into bars; (You can also not score, and simply let cool, then cut with a knife)
