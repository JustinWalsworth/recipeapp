Directions:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place steaks side by side in large casserole dish
  3. Add orange juice, cider vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce
  4. Marinate, uncovered, in the refrigerator for 45 minutes
  5. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C)
  6. Remove casserole dish from refrigerator
  7. Cover steaks with plastic wrap and let allow to come to room temperature, at least 15 minutes
  8. Place steaks on a clean work surface and generously rub with steak seasoning and black pepper
  9. Flip and cook until browned on the other side and red in the center, about 2 minutes more
  10. Transfer skillet with the steaks to the oven
  11. Bake in the preheated oven until steaks are firm and reddish-pink to lightly pink in the center, 8 to 10 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read from 130 degrees F (54 degrees C) to 140 degrees F (60 degrees C)
  12. Remove steaks from oven; season with salt
  13. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving
