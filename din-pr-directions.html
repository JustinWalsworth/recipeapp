Directions:

  1. Place prime rib roast on a plate and bring to room temperature, 2 to 4 hours
  2. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C)
  3. Combine butter, pepper, and herbes de Provence in a bowl; mix until well blended
  4. Spread butter mixture evenly over entire roast
  5. Season roast generously with kosher salt
  6. Roast the 4-pound roast in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. (If your roast is larger or smaller than 4 pounds, multiply the exact weight times 5 minutes)
  7. Turn the oven off and, leaving the roast in the oven with the door closed, let the roast sit in the oven for 2 hours
  8. Remove roast from the oven, slice, and serve
