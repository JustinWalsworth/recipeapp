Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Place chicken and potatoes into a large bowl
  3. Pour olive oil over them, and stir to coat
  4. Scatter the chicken and potato pieces in a large baking dish, or cookie sheet with sides
  5. Sprinkle with rosemary, oregano, garlic powder, salt and pepper
  6. Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven, uncovered. Baste during the last 15 minutes for extra crispness
