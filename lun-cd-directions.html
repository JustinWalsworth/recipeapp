Directions:

  1. Combine cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl; stir in milk and egg to make a batter
  2. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  3. Meanwhile, pat frankfurters dry and insert a skewer into each one
  4. Roll frankfurters in batter until well coated
  5. Fry 2 or 3 corn dogs at a time in preheated oil until lightly browned, about 3 minutes
  6. Drain on paper towels
