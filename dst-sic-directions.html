Directions:

  1. Place strawberries into the container of a blender or food processor and puree until smooth
  2. Pour into a large bowl and set aside
  3. Heat 1 1/4 cups of cream in a saucepan over medium heat until it begins to bubble at the edge of the pan
  4. Whisk sugar, egg yolks, remaining 1/4 cup cream, and corn syrup together in a large bowl
  5. Gradually pour the hot cream into egg yolk mixture, whisking constantly
  6. Return mixture to the saucepan and heat until thick enough to coat the back of a metal spoon for about 5 minutes. (Do not allow the mixture to boil)
  7. Strain custard into berry puree through a sieve; mix well and refrigerate until chilled
  8. Fill an ice cream maker with the mixture, and freeze according to the manufacturer's instructions
