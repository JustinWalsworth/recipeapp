Directions:

  1. Whisk together eggs, milk, flour, melted butter, sugar, and salt in a large bowl until smooth
  2. Heat a medium skillet or crepe pan over medium heat
  3. Grease the pan with a small amount of butter or oil applied with a brush or paper towel
  4. Using a serving spoon or small ladle, spoon about 3 tablespoons crepe batter onto the hot pan, tilting the pan to evenly coat the bottom surface
  5. Cook 1 to 2 minutes per side until golden brown
  6. Serve warm
