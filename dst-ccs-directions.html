Directions:

  1. Melt chocolate and shortening in a double boiler, stirring occasionally until smooth
  2. Insert toothpicks into tops (stem ends) of strawberries
  3. Hold a strawberry by the toothpick and dip it into the chocolate mixture
  4. Turn the strawberry upside down and insert the toothpick into a Styrofoam block or place strawberry on a sheet of waxed paper
  5. Allow chocolate coating to cool
  6. Repeat with remaining strawberries
